Crew

No Country for Old Men was directed and written by the brothers Ethan and Joel Coen.

Ethan Coen

Ethan Coen is one of the directors of No Country for Old Men. He also worked as one of the writers of the screenplay together with his brother Joel. He has won several awards of his lifespan and worked together with his older brother on most projects. He is married to Tricia Cooke and has two children with her.For more information.

Joel Coen

Joel Coen is the other director of No Country for Old Men. Just like his brother Ethan he also worked as a writer of the screenplay. Just like his younger brother he has won several awards. He is married to the actress frances McDormand and has an adopted son. For more information
