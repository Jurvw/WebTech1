Ed Tom

A law enforcement officer who doesn't get surprised by the hard realities in his field anymore. Knowing his community well, as its sheriff, he uses common sense in a race against the clock to safe Llewelyn from his predicament.

Anton Chigurh

A ruthlessly principled individual, he tries to force destiny by shear force, with no regard for the sancity of human life. Intolerable by design, he doesn't forgive.

Llewelyn Moss

An opportunistic act inserts him right into the center of an organized crime. Relying on his Vietnam days he is able to stay ahead of the dangerous elements for a bit, but not for long.

Carson Wells

A glorified hired gun for one of the parties looking for Llewelyn's bag of cash, fatally crosses paths with Anton Chigurh in his search for Llewelyn.
